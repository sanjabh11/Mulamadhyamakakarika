Help & Accessibility Information

This interactive experience explores the concept of emptiness in Buddhist philosophy through the lens of quantum physics.

Navigation Controls:

  • Previous/Next Buttons: Navigate between verses
  • Verse Dots: Jump directly to a specific verse
  • Panel Toggle (≡): On mobile, show/hide the text panel

Interaction Controls:

Each verse has unique interactive elements that demonstrate concepts through quantum physics analogies:

  • Buttons: Trigger specific actions or changes
  • Toggles: Switch between different states
  • Sliders: Adjust values continuously

Accessibility Features:

  • Text descriptions of each visualization
  • High contrast colors
  • Responsive design for all devices
  • Keyboard navigable controls

Touch Controls:

  • Swipe: Navigate between verses
  • Pinch/Spread: Zoom in/out of 3D scenes
  • Tap and Drag: Rotate 3D objects
