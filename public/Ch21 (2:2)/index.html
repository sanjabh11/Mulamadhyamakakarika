Verse 1/12

Verse 1: Arising and Ceasing

Madhyamaka Concept

Challenges inherent arising and ceasing, emphasizing emptiness and dependent origination, rejecting inherent essence (svabhāva).

Quantum Physics Parallel

Superposition: An electron exists in multiple energy levels until measured.

Accessible Explanation

Like a light switch both on and off until checked, things don't inherently start or stop—it's all perspective.
