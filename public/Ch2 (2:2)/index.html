Examination of Motion

Verse Explanation

▼

"Since the beginning of motion Cannot be conceived in any way, What gone thing, what going thing, And what non-going thing can be posited?"

Madhyamaka Concept

If motion's start is empty, then past, present, and future states of motion are also empty. This extends emptiness to all temporal categories, a Madhyamaka hallmark.

Quantum Physics Parallel

Superposition allows a particle to occupy multiple temporal states simultaneously, defying fixed labels like "gone" or "going," as in the verse.

Accessible Explanation

Imagine a clock with hands pointing everywhere at once. In quantum physics, particles can blur past, present, and future until you check them.
